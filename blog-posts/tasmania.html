Touring Tasmania

March 20, 2022

Taking a week between the end of one job and the beginning of another, it was time to see what Tassie was all about. And I was delighted!

I picked up a car from the airport and freely drove around with a rough itinerary in mind.

One of the best parts was a visit to Port Arthur. When buying a tour ticket, one is asked their last name and postcode. I secured my ticket and was wandering around the reception when I overheard the cashier asking if the two gentlemen buying their own tickets were “with the young lady” - given the same last name!

What are the chances?! What if they had gone to a different register to buy their tickets? What if the cashier hadn't commented about the same last name? They were Dad's cousins through Grandpa.

After the tour we met again at a distillery down the road and had a good chin wag getting to know each other. The surprises and chances that traveling always has in store for us.
