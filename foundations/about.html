Gabrielle Kneipp

Gabrielle is a freelance journalist, forever curious and wanting to know more.

After completing her studies in 2018, she headed abroad to further her experience in the field.

With a passion for travel, it's the diverse and amazing people she meets in new landscapes, immersing herself in cultures in many corners of the world. Spanish has been picked up along the way, as well as a touch of Arabic and French - each a work in progress!
